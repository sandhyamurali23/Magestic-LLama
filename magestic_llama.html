About Magestic Llama

Origin

Llama is thought to have originated in North America around 40 million years ago and have migrated to South America and Asia around 3 million years ago!

Ancestors

The llama is thought to have evolved from the old world camel-like animals that inhabited the regions that is today the Middle East!

Food Habits

The llama gets most of its nutrition from grass, leaves and young shoots.Llama must drink more often and llamas therefore prefer to be close to water!

Breeding

Llamas give birth to baby llamas (known as crias). The gestation period for a llama is between 11 and 12 months and the birth of the cria is usually over within half an hour!
